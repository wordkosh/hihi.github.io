Phishing

what is phishing, and how it steals your data?


Phishing is a cybercrime in which a target or targets are contacted by email, telephone or text message by someone posing as a legitimate institution to lure individuals into providing sensitive data such as personally identifiable information, banking and credit card details, and passwords.

In simple words, Phishing is a way of hacking your device and stealing all your informations just like Fishing.

For eg. When you try to trap fish by plugging food in fish net hook, it got trapped in it.

Just like that, the hacker sends you the link, and when you click that link and fill your informations all your information is got collected by the hacker.

The message that contains the is is so clear that you are not able to understand that it is fake message.

Common ways of phishing are👇👇


1. Phishing with wrong government website💁💁

Hackers make websites that are similar like official government website, and when you fill your details in that website it goes to the hackers and then they hack your device and steal all of your Data.


2. Phishing with name of Company 💻💻

In this scam the hackers sends you the mail with name of your company staff and send you a link tell you to fill this with your personal information and when you fill your details in that website it goes to the hackers and then they hack your device and steal all of your Data.

3. Phishing with giving lottery or money offers🎆🎆

Hackers send mail with lottery and other offers to you and when you try to get then it ask you to fill some informations and hack your data.

Just like these the hackers try to steal the data from other ways also by sending links in Phishing
